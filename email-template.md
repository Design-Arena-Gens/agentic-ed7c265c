   We will schedule a brief training session to familiarize your team with the new system, followed by the official go-live date.

## Email Tips for Copy-Paste Use

1. **Personalization:** Replace all bracketed fields [like this] with actual information
2. **Formatting:** Most email clients support basic Markdown-style formatting (bold, lists, etc.)
3. **Length:** Choose between the detailed or shorter version based on your audience's needs
4. **Tone:** Professional yet warm and approachable
5. **Call-to-Action:** Clear next steps with specific timelines
6. **Contact Info:** Always provide multiple ways to reach you
